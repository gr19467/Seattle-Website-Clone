-There's so many things to do here in Seattle, but here's some of our family friendly favorites.-

-Seattle Aquarium-

Located on pier 59, the Seattle Aquarium is the ninth largest aquarium in the US by attendance. It's a two minute walk from the Seattle Great Wheel. The aquarium is home to over 12,000 different animals.

-Argosy Cruises-

Argosy Cruises offers a variety of different sightseeing cruises around the Seattle Harbor. Each tour includes full narration about Seattle's history and current day. There's also a full beverage bar, snacks, and food.

-Woodland Park Zoo-

The Woodland Park Zoo manages the most live animals in Washington state. The zoo also supports 35+ wildlife conservation projects.
